Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration. Over the years, it has hosted breakthrough discussions, inspiring presentations, and cutting-edge technology showcases.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to empower the global tech community by creating an inclusive space for learning, sharing, and innovating. We believe in the power of technology to solve problems, inspire change, and shape the future.

Past Speakers

We’ve been honored to host pioneers in the tech industry, including:

  • Grace Hopper – A computer science legend who championed early programming languages.
  • Alan Kay – A visionary in user interface and object-oriented programming.
  • Margaret Hamilton – The lead software engineer for NASA’s Apollo missions.
